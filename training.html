Training and Workshops

The Brain Image library regularly offers virtual training workshops on how to use the resources available. Please reach out to bil-support@psc.edu for questions regarding training or workshops.

Spatial Omics Workshop May 12-16, 2025 The Brain Image Library (BIL), Human BioMolecular Atlas Program (HuBMAP), Common Fund Data Ecosystem (CFDE), Giotto, SPArrOW and Harpy teams held a Spatial Omics virtual workshop. Participants were introduced to spatial omics technologies, Giotto, Sparrow and Harpy tools and used PSC’s HPC System to explore real-world spatial expression data generated by the BRAIN Initiative and HuBMAP projects.
