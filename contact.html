The Brain Image Library (BIL) is a national public resource enabling researchers to deposit, analyze, mine, share and interact with large brain image datasets.

Helpdesk Support

The BIL Helpdesk is here to help you and answer your questions.

Please contact bil-support@psc.edu for assistance with submissions or services.

The BIL team can assist with technical questions, assistance depositing files in BIL including networking support, assistance retreiving data deposited in BIL, and more.

BIL Office Hours

Join the BIL team virtually each month for hands-on assistance. Come with questions about your submission process, using BIL resources, etc.

Schedule: Second Wednesday of the month 2-3PM Eastern
